Brodie's Backyard

High Quality Gardening & Landscaping Services

What We Believe

Your backyard is more than just a patch of grass — it's an extension of your home, your lifestyle, and your story. At Brodie's Backyard, we believe every outdoor space has the potential to become somewhere truly special. Whether it's a sun-soaked entertaining area, a lush garden retreat, or a space the whole family can enjoy, we bring the care, craft, and creativity to make it happen.

What We Do

From a tidy weekly mow to a full backyard transformation — we take care of the outdoor jobs so you don't have to.

Lawn Mowing & Edging

Regular mowing, clean edging, and trimming to keep your lawn looking sharp week in, week out.

Garden Maintenance

Weeding, pruning, mulching, and general garden upkeep to keep your beds tidy and thriving.

Landscaping & Design

Transforming outdoor spaces with thoughtful design — retaining walls, garden beds, and more.

Hedge & Shrub Trimming

Precision shaping of hedges, topiaries, and shrubs to give your property a clean, structured look.

Turf Laying

Supply and installation of fresh turf for new lawns, bare patches, or complete lawn restorations.

Rubbish & Green Waste Removal

After any job, big or small, we haul away all clippings, green waste, and garden rubbish. No mess left behind.
