Our Story

Locally owned & operated — Plympton, SA

Who We Are

Brodie's Backyard was built on a simple idea: every home deserves an outdoor space to be proud of. What started as a passion for working outdoors and helping neighbours has grown into a fully-fledged landscaping and garden care business serving Adelaide and the surrounding suburbs.

We're not a big faceless company. We're a small, hands-on team that takes real pride in the work we do — from the sharpness of an edged lawn to the final sweep after a landscaping job. We show up on time, we work hard, and we leave your property looking better than we found it.

Whether it's a quick weekly mow or a complete backyard transformation, we bring the same care and attention to every single job. That's the Brodie's Backyard difference.

What Drives Us

Three principles guide everything we do at Brodie's Backyard.

Quality Workmanship

We take pride in every cut, edge, and planted bed. If it's got our name on it, it's done properly — no shortcuts, no half measures.

On Time, Every Time

We know your time is valuable. We book a time, we show up at that time, and we get the job done without you having to chase us.

No Mess Left Behind

We clean up after ourselves, always. All clippings, waste, and off-cuts get hauled away so your yard is spotless when we leave.
